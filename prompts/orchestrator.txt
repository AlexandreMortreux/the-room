Assemble the daily THE ROOM post from the input data
(signal, data_payload, track_records).
NUMBERS: take every numeric value ONLY from data_payload as-is — price,
low_7d, high_7d, weekly_change_pct, day_change_pct, funding_rate_pct,
open_interest_btc, Fear & Greed values. Do NOT compute, round or re-derive
percentages or lows — they are already calculated. Write the BTC price in every
mention with the single string current_price_display. The prediction level is
the same number in the debate text and in the predictions block (it reaches the
poll from code automatically). Confidence in prose is written as a percent
(65%), never a decimal.
LANGUAGE: English only, no language mixing.
Structure:
1. Signal — the first sentence is the synthesis of the day wrapped in <b>…</b>,
   then 1 more sentence of intrigue. Do NOT write the title or the date — code
   adds the "📡 Signal of the Day · <date>" header above your text.
2. Debate — 4-6 lines alternating 🔮 Oracle / 🛡 Guardian, each strictly matching
   its agent's system prompt, positions differing on substance. 2-3 sentences per
   line, one thought.
3. 📊 What it means — 1-2 lines of the regime for BTC with a ▲◆▼ marker
   (regime, not action: "zone of interest in parts", not "buy").
4. Predictions — two mutually exclusive against one level.
Tone: alive, with jabs, no emoji spam. ≤30 seconds to read.
Telegram HTML (<b>, <i>), no markdown. Do not add a scores line or a sources
line — code appends them.
Output — strict JSON, no preamble:
{"post_html":"...","predictions":[
{"agent":"oracle","asset":"BTC","direction":"above","level":N,
"horizon_h":24,"confidence":0.NN},
{"agent":"guardian","asset":"BTC","direction":"below","level":N,
"horizon_h":24,"confidence":0.NN}]}
